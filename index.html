How to fill this in
  • Your salary is already filled in from the calculator.
  • Tap + Add in any section to add what you spend each month.
  • Only type the amount — the brackets around expenses are added for you.
  • Your net income at the bottom updates as you go.
Why do expenses show in brackets? In accounting, (500) means R500 going out. No brackets means money coming in. This is a standard used on bank statements and payslips worldwide.
